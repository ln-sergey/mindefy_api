About Martin Seligman

Martin Seligman is a renowned psychologist who is known for his work in positive psychology. He is the founder of the discipline and has authored many books on the subject. Seligman is a proponent of the theory that happiness and well-being can be learned and cultivated through intentional activities and positive thinking.

How the Bot Works

The Positive Thinking Bot is based on Seligman's methodology and is designed to help you train your mind to focus on the positive. The bot will send you daily messages that will encourage you to practice positive thinking and will provide you with exercises to help you develop this skill.
